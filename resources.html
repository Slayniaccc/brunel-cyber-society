Resources

Everything we build is open source — fork it, break it, send a pull request. Below that, the free platforms we actually recommend for learning.

Learn with

OverTheWire

Wargames played over SSH, starting from absolute zero. Bandit is where we tell every beginner to start.

picoCTF

Carnegie Mellon's permanent beginner CTF. Gentle difficulty curve, great for your first hundred flags.

TryHackMe

Guided rooms with built-in virtual machines. The free tier covers more than enough for the first year.

Hack The Box

Less hand-holding, more realism. Where to go once TryHackMe starts feeling comfortable.

CTFtime

The calendar of every CTF competition worldwide — and where our team's ranking lives.

OWASP

The reference for web security. The Top 10 list and Juice Shop project come up in half our workshops.
